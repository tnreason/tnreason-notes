    \medskip
    \textbf{Situation:}\\
    Imagine reasoning about whether a Burglary or an Earthquake appeared at your home, with access to the information whether the Alarm started, and whether the neighbors John or Mary called.
    We know, that:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item Whenever a Burglary or an Earthquake appears, the Alarm clock rings.
        \item Whenever the Alarm glock rings then we receive a call at least by one of the neighbors John and Mary.
        \item The neighbor John never calls.
    \end{itemize}
    \medskip
    \textbf{Task 1:}\\
    Define suitable Boolean variables giving a factored representation of this system and express the knowledge in corresponding formulas.\\
    \medskip \\
    \textbf{Task 2:}\\
    Find the truth table representation and the tensor representation of the formulas. \\
    \medskip \\
    \textbf{Task 3:} \\
    Decide by explicit tensor network contractions, which of these statements is entailed by our knowledge:
    \begin{itemize}
        \item When the Alarm clock rings, either a Burglary or an Earthquake happened.
        \item When the Alarm clock rings, then Mary calls.
    \end{itemize}
