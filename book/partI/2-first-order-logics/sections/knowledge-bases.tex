\section{Knowledge bases}

FOL knowledge bases without existential quantifiers are understood as contraction equations.
\begin{align*}
    \contractionof{
        \{\folexformulaof{\selindex}\left[\indvariableof{[r_{\folexformulaof{\selindex}}]}\right] \wcols \selindexin\}
    }{\indvariableof{\nodes}}
    = \onesat{\indvariableof{\nodes}}
\end{align*}
